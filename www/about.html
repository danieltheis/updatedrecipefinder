This Is Who We Are

about us

  • We are just an average group of people looking to make something that would have helped us in our college years. We have many different backgrounds but one thing we all have in common is our past struggles with eating proper meals. If we didn't go back home, we were generally eating fast food or maybe an old hot dog we found in the fridge for dinner. Putting an end to these struggles is what drove us to create this website Recipe Finder has been a collective effort of minds to shed light on how easy cooking can be if you just get familiar with what is required for cooking good meals. We believe that anyone can cook themselves a meal worthy of Instagram pictures if they just get a little nudge in creativity.
